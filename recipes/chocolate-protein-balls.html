Chocolate Protein Balls

Need some extra energy during and after your gym sessions? Here's an easy protein ball recipe that you'll definitely love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup natural peanut butter
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ¼ cup chopped dark chocolate
  • 2 tablespoons flax seeds
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chocolate-flavored protein powder
  • Optional additions:
    • shredded coconut
    • dried fruit such as cranberries or raisins
    • chopped nuts such as almonds or pecans
    • hemp seeds
    • spices such as cinnamon or pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Stir oats, peanut butter, honey, chocolate, flax seeds, chia seeds, and protein powder together in a bowl until evenly mixed.
  2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Scoop chilled mixture into balls. Keep cold until serving.
  4. Serve!
