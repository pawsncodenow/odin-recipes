Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

Bake dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips, with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown. (in an oven 350 degrees F, it should be perfectly baked in about 10 minutes)
