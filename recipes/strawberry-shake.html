Healthy Strawberry Shake Recipe

Description

Beat the summer heat with this healthy yet delicious strawberry shake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine milk, strawberries, honey, and vanilla in a blender.
  2. Blend until it looks smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
  4. Add ice to keep it cool (optional)
